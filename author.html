About this site

I made this site to introduce One Piece, a renowned Japanese cartoon. The site includes 4 parts : intro to One Piece, the character "Chopper" info, gallery and manuscript. I hope to share this work to more people.

How One Piece has changed my life

About two years ago now, I was in one of my downturn periods when I was looking for a distraction. I had heard my cousin talk about One Piece, mainly its length and great world building so I decided I'd read the manga. My goodness, I am glad I did. I read the manga in less than a year, and it has provided a great distraction for me during bad times and an amazing adventure to marvel over during my good times. And now when I think to myself that there's nothing good left, I read through some of the epic moments and remember that I still have to see Luffy turn the world upside down by becoming King of Pirates. And sometimes remembering that I still have to see that gives me the push I need.
